## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a vulnerability, please email us at [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS] and include the following information:

*   A clear and descriptive title.
*   A description of the vulnerability, including the potential impact.
*   Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
*   Any relevant code snippets or proof-of-concept.

Please do not report security vulnerabilities through public GitHub issues.

We will confirm receipt of your report within 48 hours, and will provide you with a timeline for a fix and public disclosure.
